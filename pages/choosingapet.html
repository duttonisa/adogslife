How to chose the right pet for you:

Before you begin,
you have to understand owning another life isn't just for fun. It is an investment and a long term commitment.
Then, ask yourself,
are you responsible enough to own and take care of another life, and wont neglect them.
Consider your living conditions
EG. is it suitable for me to own a big dog, if I live in a small apartment?
Research
Before you just go out and pick someone up, take the time to find what would be most suitable for you.
Love
Once you've decided you're ready to move forward, once you go out the explore, the right pet will chose you. Be patient and wait until you get a good feeling. No need to rush things.
